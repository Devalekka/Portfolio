🏆 Selected Among Top 500 Ideas & Won ₹15,000 Cash Prize — Niral Thiruvizha, Tamil Nadu

NutriSnap — AI-Powered Nutrition Tracker

Out of 24,411 ideas submitted at Niral Thiruvizha, NutriSnap was selected among the Top 500 ideas and awarded a ₹15,000 cash prize. NutriSnap is an AI-powered mobile app that makes nutrition tracking effortless — just snap a photo, speak, or type what you eat, and the app does the rest. It identifies food items using AI and instantly estimates nutritional values, removing the hassle of manual logging. With streak tracking, reward systems, and friend leaderboards, the app keeps users motivated and consistent. Supporting both Tamil and English with voice interaction, NutriSnap is built to be fast, smart, and accessible for everyone.
